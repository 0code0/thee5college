Principal Message

Education is a powerful tool which shapes and moulds the character and behaviour of an individual and hence strengthen the society and nation at large .It inculcates human values like peace, brotherhood, harmony, happiness etc. for a prosperous and enlightened human society which is the ultimate goal of providing good education.

With a mission to transform the rural society of the region the E5 group management has decided to establish a women college with a vision to empower girls in the rural society through a qualitative, innovative and value based education.

To empower the students with knowledge, wisdom and skills the college will focus equally on academic and extra-curricular activities. It will provide a conducive and congenial environment for the physical, mental and emotional growth of students. Main motive of the college will be developing life values like patience,perseverance, dedication, devotion, discipline etc. by arranging experienced and dedicated faculty. It will be my pleasure and honour to be the founder principal of this prestigious newly established institution for women empowerment. From its inception the college will be committed to develop excellence in every sphere. Be it academic, cultural, sports or professional advancement.

I assure that this college will play a meaningful role in the competitive times ahead and scale new heights, translating the dreams of the people of the area into reality and bring education in this rural area. I pray for future growth and prosperity of the college and wish the managements, staff and students all success in the years to come.

Principal

Prof. Pratap Singh Rohilla
